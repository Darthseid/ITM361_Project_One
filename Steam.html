Bio for Kelen

Kelen, the footpad.

In a small cottage on the outskirts of Thallasa, there was a strange man who never smiled and seemed to blend in with the background. The owner of the cottage was Kelen, a master cut burglar who retired from the business several years ago when his partner in crime died in a gig gone wrong. The only memento he had left of his partner was a peculiar, blue dagger that allowed the pair to escape from any danger. Any danger that is, except from a rune trap that disintegrated an unaware thief.

Today, however was Kelen's turn to be unaware, because after an afternoon of fishing. He returned to his modest, yet beautifully adorned cottage to find everything ransacked, including the dagger. He tracked the bandits who did this, but after snapping the necks of their leader, the dagger trail has gone cold. The only option Kelen had was to get back into business and earn himself a new dagger, one picked lock at a time.
